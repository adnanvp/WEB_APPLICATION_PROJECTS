Spicy Thai Noodles

Posted on September 8, 2025 by Adnan
These spicy Thai noodles are bursting with flavor and take only 20 minutes to prepare! A perfect dish for busy nights.
Ingredients:
  • 200g rice noodles
  • 2 tbsp soy sauce
  • 1 tbsp sesame oil
  • 1 tsp chili flakes
  • Vegetables of choice
  • Fresh cilantro
Instructions:
  1. Cook noodles according to package instructions.
  2. Stir-fry vegetables in sesame oil, add soy sauce and chili flakes.
  3. Toss noodles with sauce and vegetables. Garnish with cilantro.

Classic French Toast

Posted on September 6, 2025 by Adnan
Simple, sweet, and a weekend favorite. This classic French toast recipe is perfect for breakfast or brunch!
Ingredients:
  • 4 slices of bread
  • 2 eggs
  • 1/2 cup milk
  • 1 tsp cinnamon
  • 1 tsp vanilla extract
  • Butter for frying
Instructions:
  1. Whisk eggs, milk, cinnamon, and vanilla together.
  2. Dip bread slices into mixture and fry in butter until golden.
  3. Serve with maple syrup and berries.

Avocado Toast with Poached Eggs

Posted on September 1, 2025 by Adnan
Creamy avocado, perfectly poached eggs, and crunchy toastâ€”this is a breakfast that will never go out of style.
Ingredients:
  • 2 slices sourdough bread
  • 1 ripe avocado
  • 2 eggs
  • Salt and pepper to taste
  • Chili flakes & microgreens (optional)
Instructions:
  1. Toast bread slices until golden.
  2. Mash avocado, season with salt and pepper, spread on toast.
  3. Poach eggs and place them on avocado toast. Sprinkle with chili flakes and microgreens.
